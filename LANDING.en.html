// the problem

The problem isn't speed.
It's direction.

The traditional workflow was slow — but it knew how to steer. PRDs. Sprints. Reviews. Approvals. Slow, with direction.

Then AI arrived with the promise of speed. And most people put a more powerful engine on the same conveyor belt.

Faster. Same wrong direction.

Powerful AIs generating generic outputs — because the model doesn't know your product. It's not technical hallucination. It's lack of context. Your AI knows products. It doesn't know your product.

Speed without direction isn't progress.
It's accelerated risk.
