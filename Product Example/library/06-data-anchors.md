**North-star metric:**

**Autonomous Resolution Rate**
- **Definition:** % of chatbot conversations that reach resolution without human escalation. Formula: (conversations resolved autonomously) / (total conversations started) × 100. Resolution = autonomous when no human agent is assigned within 24h AND user does not reopen within 72h.
- **Source:** Metabase — Chatbot KPIs dashboard (refreshes Sunday midnight)
- **Owner:** Ana Souza (PM)
- **Baseline (Q1 2026):** 41%
- **Target (Q2 2026):** 60%
- **Gap:** 19pp
- **Measurement cadence:** Weekly, every Monday
- **Freshness:** Valid. Re-pull at Q2 midpoint (2026-05-15) and end of Q2 (2026-06-30)

**Supporting metrics:**

**Human Escalation Rate**
- **Definition:** Inverse of autonomous resolution. % of conversations assigned to a human agent.
- **Baseline:** 59% | **Target:** ≤ 40%
- **Source:** Same Metabase dashboard as above

**P90 First-Response Time**
- **Definition:** 90th percentile time from conversation start to first substantive bot response.
- **Baseline:** 4.2 minutes | **Target:** < 60 seconds (for conversations that resolve autonomously)
- **Note:** This metric is a secondary KR. Primary focus is resolution rate, not response speed.

**Cost-per-Ticket**
- **Current:** $2.10 | **Company target:** $0.80
- **Note:** Not directly owned by this PM — Finance tracks this. Use for context and framing only; do not commit to this metric in product decisions without Finance alignment.
