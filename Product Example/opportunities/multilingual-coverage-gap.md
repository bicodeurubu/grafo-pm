# Opportunity: Multilingual Coverage Gap

LATAM customers (Brazil and Mexico primarily) represent 22% of Acme's customer base but generate 35% of human escalations. The root cause: the chatbot was trained exclusively on English data and has a resolution rate of only 9% for Portuguese and Spanish conversations.

**Evidence:**

**Quantitative:**
- [[intelligence/data/chatbot-funnel-q1]] — Portuguese/Spanish resolution rate: 9% vs 47% for English
- Internal CRM data: LATAM churn rate 2.1x higher than North America (attributed to support quality in sales exit surveys)

**Qualitative:**
- [[intelligence/research/interview-synthesis-q1]] — 3 LATAM interviews; all 3 users switched to email support and described the chatbot as "useless"

**Connected outcome:** [[outcomes/increase-autonomous-resolution-q2]]

**Why this is lower priority than [[opportunities/intent-classification-gap]] in Q2:**
See [[intelligence/decisions/prioritize-intent-over-multilingual]]. Intent classification, if fixed, improves the 78% English-speaking majority first and has faster time-to-value. Multilingual is Q3 target.

- No solutions created yet — Q3 target
